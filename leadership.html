Leadership & Management

Building teams that move fast, think clearly, and actually enjoy
the work

15+ years of guiding creative teams, mentoring designers, and delivering award-winning projects. Here's how I approach leadership, collaboration, and building design cultures that empower people to do their best work.

My Approach

Leadership Philosophy

🎯

Vision with Flexibility

I set clear creative direction while giving designers space to explore. The best solutions come when talented people have room to think, not when they're micromanaged into a corner.

🌱

Growth over Perfection

Junior designers learn by doing, not watching. I create safe environments to experiment, fail forward, and build confidence through real projects with appropriate scaffolding.

🤝

Collaboration as Default

Great work is a team sport. I facilitate workshops, critique sessions, and cross-functional alignment—not to reach consensus, but to make better-informed decisions together.

⚡

Ship and Iterate

Perfect is the enemy of done. I help teams balance craft with delivery, knowing when to push for excellence and when to ship and learn from real user feedback.

How I Work

My Leadership Process

Phase 1: Discovery

Understanding the Challenge

Before assigning work, I dig into the business context, user needs, and technical constraints. I run stakeholder alignment sessions to ensure everyone's working from the same brief.

Stakeholder Interviews Research Synthesis Brief Writing
📋
Phase 2: Team Formation

Strategic Team Building

I match designers to projects based on growth opportunities, not just skills. Junior designers get stretch assignments with senior support. Everyone has clear roles and ownership areas.

Skill Assessment Pairing Strategy Kickoff Workshops
👥
Phase 3: Guidance & Critique

Active Mentorship

Weekly design critiques with structured frameworks. I teach designers how to defend their work, give actionable feedback, and elevate ideas through iteration. 1:1s focus on career development, not just status updates.

Design Critiques 1:1 Coaching Skill Workshops
💬
Phase 4: Quality & Delivery

Shipping Excellence

I conduct final reviews to ensure consistency, accessibility, and brand alignment. After launch, we do team retrospectives to capture learnings and celebrate wins.

QA Reviews Retrospectives Documentation
