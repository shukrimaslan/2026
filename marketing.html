Case Study 01

Scaling Social Engagement Through Content Strategy

Transformed JumpFromPaper's social presence by shifting from static product posts to story-driven, personality-led content that highlights the brand's unique 2D illusion style.

Objective

Increase brand visibility and engagement across social platforms for Jump From Paper, targeting Gen Z and young millennials interested in playful, fashion-forward accessories.

Strategy

Shift from static product posts to story-driven, personality-led content that highlights the brand's unique 2D illusion style.

Execution
  • Developed a consistent content direction across Instagram and TikTok
  • Introduced short-form video content focusing on styling, daily use, and visual illusions
  • Designed scroll-stopping creatives aligned with a vibrant, playful tone
  • Experimented with trending formats and platform-native storytelling
Results
📈
Improved engagement rate across key posts
🔄
Increased content shareability and saves
✨
Strengthened brand identity consistency
Your Role
Led content direction, visual execution, and platform strategy

Case Study 05

Expanding Digital Engagement Through Content Ecosystem

Increased brand visibility, strengthen the digital presence and engagement. Shifted to content-driven approach aligned with audience behavior.

Objective

Increase audience engagement and strengthen digital presence across SYOK’s content ecosystem, while making content more discoverable and platform-friendly.

Strategy

Shift from traditional broadcast-style promotion to a digital-first content ecosystem, where content is snackable, shareable and platform-native. Focus on turning SYOK from just a listening platform into a content-driven entertainment hub.

Execution
  • Translated radio and long-form content into short-form, social-friendly formats
  • Designed engaging visuals and motion content tailored for Instagram and digital platforms
  • Introduced content formats such as highlight clips, personality-driven content (DJs, hosts) and entertainment snippets optimised for scrolling behaviour
  • Ensured consistent visual identity across different shows and segments
  • Supported content rollout strategies to maintain frequency and relevance
Results
📈
Increased engagement on digital content across platforms
🛠️
Improved content discoverability beyond traditional listeners
🥇
Strengthened SYOK’s positioning as a modern, digital-first entertainment platform
Your Role
Contributed to shaping a more platform-native content direction, led creative execution and content adaptation for digital platforms
